## Welcome to JFrog Fly

Getting started with JFrog Fly is quick and straightforward. This guide will walk you through the essential setup steps to get your team up and running.

<Info>
  **Estimated time:** Complete setup takes approximately 10-15 minutes.
</Info>

---

## Setup Journey

Follow these three steps to get fully operational with JFrog Fly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account & Team Setup" icon="user-plus">
    Create your JFrog Fly account and configure your team workspace.
    
    <Card title="Account Setup Guide" icon="arrow-right" href="/getting-started/account-setup" />
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Git Repositories" icon="code-branch">
    Link your Git repositories to enable automatic artifact tracking and traceability.
    
    <Card title="Git Connection Guide" icon="arrow-right" href="/getting-started/git-connection" />
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install Desktop App" icon="desktop">
    Install the Fly Desktop App to integrate with your local development environment.
    
    <Card title="Desktop App Setup" icon="arrow-right" href="/getting-started/desktop-app" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="GitHub or GitLab Account" icon="github">
    A source control account for repository connections
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin Access" icon="key">
    Admin access to repositories you want to connect (for initial setup)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Supported OS" icon="laptop">
    macOS, Windows, or Linux for the Desktop App
  </Card>
  <Card title="Package Managers" icon="box">
    Your preferred package managers installed (npm, pip, maven, etc.)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## What You'll Achieve

After completing the setup, you'll be able to:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Manage Artifacts Centrally" icon="database">
    Store all your packages, containers, and dependencies in your Fly Registry with automatic versioning and traceability.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Enable Agentic Workflows" icon="robot">
    Use AI-powered commands in your IDE to manage releases, search artifacts, and configure pipelines.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Automate CI/CD Integration" icon="gears">
    Seamlessly integrate Fly with your existing GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or other build systems.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search Semantically" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Find any artifact or release using natural language queries instead of memorizing exact names.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
